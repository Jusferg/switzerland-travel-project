Why Grindelwald?

Grindelwald, the best place to ski

As one of Switzerland's most popular winter ski resorts, Grindelwald is the perfect place to head for world class skiing and snowboarding. But there are plenty of outdoor activities in the summer months, too. It's a town that offers something for everyone no matter what time of year you visit.

From hiking Grindelwald to the Tissot Grindelwald Cliff Walk, to skiing or taking a cable car, Grindelwald will keep you busy all year round. If you're planning a trip to Switzerland, Grindelwald is a must-visit destination.
